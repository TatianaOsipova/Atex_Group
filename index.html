About us

Our company Atex Group is a young perspective company with full logistics services.
Even though our company has not been in the logistics services market for so long, we already have a solid team consisting of professional drivers, dedicated 24-hour dispatchers, and a management team that organizes the smooth operation of the company, namely timely preventive maintenance and repair of trucks, conducting training to improve the skills of both drivers and the managerial staff.
Our core values are trust, respect, security, the desire to develop, and, of course, satisfy the needs of our customers by providing them with logistics services at the highest level.
